Our Mission

Here at Nine of Coffee our mission is to bring you delicious magical beverages based on Tarot Cards in an enviornment that is fun and welcoming to all. We also work hard to support our local community and the enviorment. How do we do this? Here are a few ways:

Ethical Sourcing

Our coffee is ethically sourced from farmer's collectives who enforce fair pay for their workers and is fair trade certified. It's organic, shade-grown and bird-friendly.
